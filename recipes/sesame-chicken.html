Sesame Chicken

Yummy chicken on white rice, a good, healthy and declicious meal to eat during any time of day! I prefer less seeds but its still good either way.

Describing the Chicken

First off, this is a very easy meal to cook, only needing few ingredients, and is really tasty in the end. Its great for mom's who have all their time spent on their kids, dad's who can't cook super well, and for anyone who wants a good tasty meal quickly. Its so simple even kids can cook it as well!

Overall, its a sweet and salty meal, that gives good protien and calories to your day. The rice is also nice and provides a sticky and soft contrast to the chicken. The seeds give it a good crunch too! It does take a few hours, but using a slow cooker, can be done during the work day!

You want to cook the chiken and then begin with the sauce and rice. After mixing the sauce part, you want to cool it in the fridge during cooking of the chicken. The rice can be made in a rice cooker, its the easiest way. Just pop it in for 20 minutes. After the chicken is done, marinate it with the sauce. Then you want to season it with salt, pepper, and (of course) sesame seeds! Finally lie it on top of the rice and serve it!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons dried minced onion
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 3 tablespoons water
  • salt, pepper, and sesame seeds

Directions

  1. Spread the chicken in slow cooker.
  2. Stir the honey, soy sauce, ketchup, minced onion, vegetable oil, and garlic powder in a bowl.
  3. Pour and marinate the chicken in the slow cooker, and start cooking the chicken for about 3-4 hours.
  4. While the chicken is cooking, take your rice and put it into a rice cooker for 20 minutes.
  5. After the chicken is done, remove it onto a cutting board. Then proceed to stir water and cornstarch and pour it into the cooker with the sauce.
  6. Cook the sauce on high for 10 minutes until thickened.
  7. Cut the chicken into bite sized peices and stir it into the sauce.
  8. Finally, put it onto the rice and sprinkle as much salt, pepper, and/or sesame seeds onto it.
  9. Serve!
